---
title: Globally Ignore Account
description: Opt out of Answer Overflow entirely
---

Use `/manage-account` and click **Globally ignore account** to completely opt out of Answer Overflow.

<Warning>
This is permanent and will:
- Delete all your stored messages
- Stop indexing any future messages
- Stop tracking analytics for your account
</Warning>

## Undo

Changed your mind? Use `/manage-account` and click **Stop ignoring account** to restore your account.
