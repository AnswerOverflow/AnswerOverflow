## 1. Add the bot

[Add Answer Overflow to your server](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=958907348389339146&permissions=328565083201&scope=bot+applications.commands)

<Warning>
Answer Overflow needs all requested permissions to work properly. Don't disable any of them.
</Warning>

## 2. Enable indexing

Go to your [dashboard](https://answeroverflow.com/dashboard), open the Channels tab, and check "Indexing enabled" for any channel you want indexed.

Content will start appearing within 24 hours.

## 3. What's next?

- Read the [overview](/) to understand how Answer Overflow works
- Follow the [recommended setup guide](/recommended-setup) to get the most out of your indexed channels
