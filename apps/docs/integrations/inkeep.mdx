title: Inkeep

---

Answer Overflow partners with [Inkeep](https://inkeep.com?utm=answeroverflow) to bring AI-powered answers to your community.

Inkeep pulls from your Discord chat history and other sources to give users fast, accurate answers. You can also embed an Inkeep chat widget on your Answer Overflow pages, letting visitors ask follow-up questions without leaving the page.
