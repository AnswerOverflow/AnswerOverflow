---
title: Inkeep Integration
description: Learn how to setup AI answers for your server using InKeep
---

We've partnered with [InKeep](https://inkeep.com?utm=answeroverflow) to provide AI answers for your server. InKeep is able to pull from your server's chat history, along with other sources in order to provide fast accurate answers to your users.

Along with this, you are able to set up an embedded chat on your Answer Overflow pages so that people are able to follow up on threads with the full power of InKeep.
